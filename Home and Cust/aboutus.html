About Us

The E-Waste Monitoring System streamlines e-waste management through three modules: Collect, Monitor, and Recycle. Notably, it offers distinct logins for organisations (customers - private and government organisations), employees (service-provider), and waste collection workers, ensuring tailored access and functionality.

Collect: Periodic scheduling for e-waste collections is established for the organisations, fostering a systematic approach to e-waste management. Users are allowed to submit requests for e-waste collection. The platform ensures timely communication through upcoming collection alerts, notifying organisations about scheduled e-waste collections. An analytics tool is integrated to generate insights into e-waste trends and patterns, facilitating informed decision-making.

Monitor: It enhances e-waste management by offering real-time visibility and control. Inventory features provide efficient oversight, offering real-time insights into collected electronic items. Live GPS tracking monitors e-waste movement, ensuring transparency and accountability from collection to recycling. The system maintains a comprehensive database of reports on schedules, collected quantities, and recycling centre locations in India.

Recycle: The Recycle module streamlines the process of identifying suitable recycling centres for organisations, while an acknowledgment system on the employee side receives a notification upon successful e-waste drop-off, fostering a sense of completion and adherence to proper disposal practices. A feedback mechanism is also integrated. Users are encouraged to provide feedback on the entire process of e-waste disposal, promoting a culture of continuous improvement.
